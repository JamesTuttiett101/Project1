The Cause

Brain tumours are the biggest cancer killer of people in their younger years.

Yet those most affected often feel invisible in the wider conversation. Brain tumour research has historically received a fraction of the funding given to other cancers ‚Äî and the survival rates reflect that.

The Brain Tumour Charity is the world's leading brain tumour research funder. Every pound raised goes directly towards giving families more time, more hope, and better outcomes. This evening exists because that needs to change, and because the best way to drive change is to bring people together properly.

Unique food. Great music. Good people. All for a cause that needs more attention.
