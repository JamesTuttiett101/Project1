Anatomy of a Transformation Deal

Enterprise AI Integration & Commercial Redesign

1. The Situation

A leading healthcare insurance provider was struggling to roll out Microsoft 365 Copilot. The rollout was stalled due to severe data security bottlenecks (up to 7-month delays for GDPR compliance checks on prompts) and heavy employee resistance. They were paying for enterprise licences that simply weren't delivering value.

2. The Stakeholder Map

Partnering directly with the C-suite, I had to shift the commercial conversation. The client didn't need to "buy more software" or standard staff augmentation‚Äîthey needed an enterprise-wide change management and automation programme to unblock their own investment.

3. The Commercial Pivot

We pivoted the deal structure away from traditional 'Time & Materials' to a blended, outcome-focused squad model. We deployed AI-enabled consultants across Change Management, Data, and Software Engineering. They built secure automation frameworks, custom AI tools (Gemini, Power Automate), and established helpdesks to drive actual human adoption, rather than just technical implementation.

4. The Outcome

This pivot unlocked major cash savings, operational efficiency, and a complete reversal in employee adoption across the UK operations.

233% Increase in active Copilot usage (scaled from 300 to 1,000+ users).
90% Of employees reported immediate daily productivity gains.
20 Mins Saved per task via bespoke prompt tools and automations.
30% Reduction in manual task tracking and reporting times.
